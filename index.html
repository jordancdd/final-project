Suzanne Collins is an American author best known for her work in young adult fiction. She was born on August 10, 1962, in Hartford, Connecticut. She has received numerous awards and honors for her work and is widely celebrated for her ability to engage young readers and tackle thought-provoking themes within her novels. Her writing has had a significant impact on the young adult literature genre.
